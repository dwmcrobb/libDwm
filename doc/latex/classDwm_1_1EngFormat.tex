\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class to format numbers in an engineering-\/type form. 

Instead of printing numerical powers of 10, we print \textquotesingle{}K\textquotesingle{} (kilo), \textquotesingle{}M\textquotesingle{} (mega), \textquotesingle{}m\textquotesingle{} (milli), \textquotesingle{}u\textquotesingle{} (micro), et. al.

Example\+:

uint32\+\_\+t v = 123456; std\+::cout \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}\texorpdfstring{$<$}{<} \doxylink{classDwm_1_1EngFormat}{Dwm\+::\+Eng\+Format(v)} \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}\texorpdfstring{$<$}{<} std\+::endl;

would print\+:

123.\+5K

{\ttfamily precision} is the maximum precision (places including those to the left of the decimal point) to print. {\ttfamily width} is the minimum field width to print; we right-\/justify and fill with spaces.
